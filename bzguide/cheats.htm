Cheat Codes

To be able to enter cheat codes you need to bring up the Console View. It is important to note that the game is still continuing while you do this, and you will not be able to retaliate if anyone attacks you. It is therefore advisable to make sure that you are in a semi-safe place before attempting this.

To activate Console View hold down the CTRL key and press the ~ key. Once the console is up you will be able to toggle any of the following cheats by typing in "game.cheat XXXX". Do not include the ""s and replace XXXX with any of the following cheat codes. Once you have hit enter you will get an audio message saying that the cheat has either been activated or deactivated.
 

BZTNT Unlimited Ammo
BZBODY Unlimited Armor
BZFREE Unlimited Scrap
BZVIEW Satellite View
BZRADAR Map Revealed
