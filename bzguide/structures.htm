Structural Information

When planning out a base it is very important to know the cost, and requirements, of not only your Units, but also of your Structures. As many units have specific structural requirements, being able to plan out the units that you will use in advance can be drastically important. The following is meant to give you some of the key information on each structure so that you can properly plan out your bases' layout without directly telling you what to do, as you'll have to make choices on the fly.
