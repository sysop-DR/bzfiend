Battlezone II: Combat Commander (PC)

Battlezone II is a strategy title with enough action to keep your palms sweaty and your adrenaline pumping. In the not too distant future, the Scion, a mysterious alien race from the nether regions of the universe, have set up camp on Pluto to prep themselves for an apparent attack on Earth. As Lieutenant John Cooke, the fate of the free world is (partially) in your hands, and it's up to you to help the ISDF (International Space Defense Force) defeat these extraterrestrial terrorists and send them back home with their proverbial tails between their legs.  (Click on the Image to the Right for a Strategy Guide)
